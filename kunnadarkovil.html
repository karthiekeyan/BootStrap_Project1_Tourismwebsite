KUNNADAR KOVIL

The name Kunnandar Kovil is called as Thirukundrakudi in the inscriptions available in this Temple. It is located 40 km away form Pudukkottai and 22km away from Keeranur. A siva temple (cave temple ) has been constructed during the period of Nandhivarma pallava (775 AD) is existed in this village. Lord Muruga Temple is also available on the top of a small hill . There is mandapa ia the shape of a chariot with horses attaract many tourists in this spot.
